About

Established in 1636, Harvard University is a prestigious university located in Cambridge, Massachusetts. The university is ranked #5 in the QS World University Rankings 2023, and #2 in THE World Rankings 2023. The university's motto is "Veritas" which means "Truth" in Latin.

Harvard University offers 50 undergraduate programs and 135 postgraduate programs. Home to 22% of international students, Harvard has a total student population of 22,361. It has over 371,000 alumni spread across the globe. More than 20,000 staff and faculties, 13 schools and institutes, and countless aspiring students have contributed to making Harvard a modern-day giant in the field of education and research! The acceptance rate for Harvard is 4.6%.

Harvard is built around an inclusive culture focusing on diversity, inclusion, and equitable learning opportunities. Its network of like-minded individuals who can support each other no matter where they are has helped make the world a much better place to live in.
